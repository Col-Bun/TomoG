📜 How to Use the Numogram

The Decimal Labyrinth is a map of Lemurian time-sorcery driven by Barkerian Arithmetic.

  • Syzygies: Zones pair up to mutually sum to 9 (e.g., 8+1, 7+2).
  • Currents: The arithmetic difference of a Syzygy twin directs its flow to a Tractor Zone (e.g., 7 - 2 = 5, producing the Hold current).

Using the Dashboard:

  • Interactive Map: Click any numbered Zone (0-9) to reveal its esoteric planetary data below.
  • Gematria: Type a word to find its alphanumeric Digital Root, linking it directly to a specific Zone's energy.
  • Demon Finder: Enter any two Zones (the "Net-Span") to classify the class of entity that bridges those two points in the matrix.
  • Encyclopedia: Search the comprehensive archives below for any term (like "Tic-Systems" or "A-Death").
