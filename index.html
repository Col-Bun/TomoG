The Numogram

Pandemonium is the complete system of Lemurian demonism and time sorcery. It consists of two principal components: Numogram (time-map) and Matrix (listing the names, numbers, and attributes of the demons).

The system is constructed according to immanent criteria latent in decimal numeracy, and involves only basic arithmetical operations.

The Numogram, or Decimal Labyrinth, is composed of ten zones (numbered 0-9) and their interconnections. These zones are grouped into five pairs (syzygies) by nine-sum twinning.

Click on any of the Zones in the map to reveal its esoteric and planetary data.

Zone Zero

The Numeral 0

The figure ‘0' designates the number zero, anterior to the distinction odd/even, and also to the determination of primes (zeroth prime = 1). Zero is the only natural number that is indivisible by one. The division of any number by zero produces an infinity.

  • Digital Cumulation: Zero cumulates to zero.
  • Numeric Keypad Direction: Anomalous.

With no other number does arithmetical function cross so imperceptibly into religious and philosophical abstraction. There is a mathematico-cosmic continuum connecting the numeral zero to concepts of nullity, nihility, nothingness, absence, emptiness, void, vacuum, and neutrality.

The Zeroth Planet: Sol

The Sun (Sol-0) is by far the largest body in the Solar-system. It is approximately 4.5 billion years old, roughly half-way through its normal existence. After the completion of this phase it is destined to expand into a Red Giant.

System Notes

Zone-0 is the first of two zones mutually composing the Plex-region of the Numogram. Its Syzygetic-twin is Zone-9. This 9+0 Syzygy is carried by the demon Uttunul.

  • Tantrism: Mu Tantrism plots Zone-0 intensities onto the Coccygeal level of the spine.

Zone One

The Numeral 1

The figure ‘1' designates the number one, first odd number, and the zeroth prime. One is the lowest cardinal number, and the first ordinal. It has two basic cardinal values: the elementary (atom, unit) and the whole (completeness, unity).

Within monotheistic cultures One attains a supreme dignity, identifying God directly with 'the One'.

Mercury is the innermost planet of the Solar-system. The mercurian year is also the swiftest. Due to its long day Mercury has semi-permanent light and dark sides. In Roman mythology Mercury is known as the messenger of the gods.
